What is a WhatsApp MD Bot?

When people say "the use of a WhatsApp MD bot", they mean what it’s used for and why it’s useful.

A WhatsApp MD (Multi-Device) bot is basically a program that connects to WhatsApp using the multi-device protocol (the same one your phone and WhatsApp Web use) so it can send, receive, and process messages without needing the phone to stay online.
